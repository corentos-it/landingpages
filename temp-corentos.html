Overview of our ERP-Modules

With the modules for all areas of your processes, you can work profitably and thus increase the success of your company.

Financial accounting

Our financial accounting solution ensures that you always have a clear overview of your company's key figures. Our secure financial accounting software, designed according to the latest standards, allows you to work efficiently and reliably

Merchandise management

The merchandise management system is the ideal centerpiece for your automated business processes. Thanks to its wide range of additional modules and functions, it adapts perfectly to your specific requirements.

Production

The modules for production and manufacturing increase transparency and planning capability within your production chain. This not only improves your efficiency, but also your productivity by making production processes paperless, direct and clearer for all employees.

Logistics & Shipping

With the logistics and dispatch system module, you can optimize your intralogistics, resulting in increased efficiency. This not only saves costs, but also guarantees faster shipping for your customers.
