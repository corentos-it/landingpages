Our Mission​

At the Passport-Scanner the customer is the focus. Our goal is to help hotels worldwide optimize their check-in processes and increase guest satisfaction. We believe in the power of technology to simplify workflows and minimize errors, giving hoteliers more time to focus on the well-being of their guests.

This is how the passport scanner works

1- Download App: Download the Passport Scanner app from the App Store or Google Play.

2- Furnish: Follow the simple steps to set up the app and connect to your PMS.

3- Scan: Scan your guests' passports or ID cards with your mobile phone.

4- Data transfer: The scanned data is automatically and securely transferred to your PMS.

Easy configuration

The Passport-Scanner is designed to be effortlessly integrated into your existing hotel management system. The installation process is straightforward and does not require any special technical knowledge. The software is ready for use in just a few steps and you can start scanning ID documents straight away.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here you will find answers to frequently asked questions about integrating the Passport-Scanners:

1. Which systems are supported?

The passport scanner is compatible with all common hotel management systems (PMS). You can find a list of supported systems in our documentation.

2. Do I need additional hardware?

No, all you need is a standard cell phone. No additional devices are required.

3. How long does installation take?

Installation usually only takes a few minutes. The exact duration depends on your system and your specific requirements.

4. Can I adapt the software to my needs?

Yes, the passport scanner is very flexible and can be easily adapted to the specific needs of your operation.

5. What do I do if I have problems with the integration?

Our support team is always available to help you resolve any issues. You can reach us using the contact form on our website or by email.
